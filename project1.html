Standard Human Mesh Recovery Method
2026 · computational image / research / visual essay

This project explores human mesh recovery as a computational process of reconstructing 3D body structure from 2D visual input.

1. Concept

The system operates through parametric modelling of the human body, translating visual signals into structured geometric representations.

2. Method

The recovery process relies on regression-based estimation of pose and shape parameters, mapped onto a deformable mesh model.

3. Output

The output is a dense 3D mesh which can be used for animation, reconstruction, and spatial analysis.
